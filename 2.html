    History & Future

    I want to walk the path of a venture with the belief that venture is the only way to live in Korea.
    I had two businessmen I respected.It was my uncle who worked in the electricity business in Changwon Industrial Complex and my father who worked in the heavy industry.
    They were both working day and night for home and for the country, but they grew up suffering from sluggish manufacturing, mid- and high-end businesses and the economic downturn.
    At that time, I had the idea that the future of Korea was not in the factory chimney, but in creative ideas.
    After entering college in science and engineering, I flew to San Jose, the home of the venture, with the determination to become a venture businessman.
    With the help of my friend, I went to Korean startups such as Siddbird and Olgagazer as well as large companies such as Facebook and Apple to feel the venture.
    What became clearer in my heart after I came back to Korea was that small technology could enrich the world. I am planning to create a new company by cultivating the special studies of computer engineering information and communication technology and venture studies that I majored in.
    I want to create new value by fusing and connecting existing ideas.
    The venture company that I want to create and dream of is a network platform company that combines Facebook, apple and Amazon.
    I want to build a platform that brings together all the world's leading companies such as venture companies and start-ups.
    If Silicon Valley in the U.S. exists offline, we want to create a huge network platform company that allows everything in Silicon Valley to be done online.
    A variety of start-up companies in the world want to create an online Silicon Valley where they listen to each other, collaborate and coexist.
    I'm trying to create a world where you can create new companies anytime, anywhere, with technology and ideas beyond your country, your school and your background.
    The company that I'm going to grow up in will be at the center of this world.
    The platform company I will grow is not just a profit-seeking company, but a company that contributes to the prosperity and coexistence of society.
    Furthermore, it will change biased nationalism, and a new free economic market will be pioneered
